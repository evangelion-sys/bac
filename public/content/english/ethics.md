## 1. Key Vocabulary (The Magic Words)

To understand the texts, you need to know the heroes and villains of the business world!

### The Villains (Unethical Practices)
*   **Corruption (الفساد):** Dishonest or fraudulent conduct by those in power.
*   **Bribery (الرشوة):** Giving money or gifts to influence someone's actions.
*   **Counterfeiting / Forgery (التزوير / التقليد):** Making fake copies of products (like fake bags or medicines).
*   **Smuggling (التهريب):** Moving goods illegally into or out of a country.
*   **Nepotism (المحسوبية / المعريفة):** Favoring relatives or friends, especially by giving them jobs.
*   **Embezzlement (الاختلاس):** Stealing money that you have been trusted with.
*   **Money Laundering (تبييض الأموال):** Concealing the origins of illegally obtained money.

### The Heroes (Ethical Practices)
*   **Ethics / Morals (الأخلاق):** Moral principles that govern a person's behavior.
*   **Integrity / Honesty (النزاهة / الصدق):** The quality of being honest and having strong moral principles.
*   **Transparency (الشفافية):** Operating in a way that creates openness between managers and employees/public.
*   **Accountability (المساءلة):** Being responsible for your actions and decisions.
*   **Probity (الاستقامة):** The quality of having strong moral principles; honesty and decency.

## 2. Essential Grammar (The Spells)

These grammar structures are frequently tested in the "Language Outcomes" section.

### 1. Expressing Wish and Regret (I wish / If only)
*   **Present Wish (Regret about a present situation):**
    *   *Rule:* Subject + wish + Subject + **Past Simple**
    *   *Example:* I wish corruption **disappeared** from our society. (It hasn't disappeared yet).
*   **Past Regret (Regret about a past action):**
    *   *Rule:* Subject + wish + Subject + **Past Perfect** (had + past participle)
    *   *Example:* I wish the government **had passed** stricter laws last year. (They didn't pass them).
*   **Future Desire (Desire for a change in behavior):**
    *   *Rule:* Subject + wish + Subject + **would + stem** (verb without 'to')
    *   *Example:* I wish people **would stop** buying counterfeit goods.

### 2. Expressing Strong Advice (It's high time / It's about time)
Used to say that something should have been done already.
*   *Rule:* It's high time + Subject + **Past Simple**
*   *Example:* It's high time the authorities **took** severe measures against smugglers.

### 3. Conditionals (Provided that / As long as)
These mean "only if" (بشرط أن).
*   *Rule:* Provided that / As long as + **Present Simple**, Subject + **will + stem** (Future)
*   *Example:* **Provided that** companies **respect** ethical codes, the economy **will prosper**.
