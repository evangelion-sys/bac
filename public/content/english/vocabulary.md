## 1. Word Formation (Prefixes and Suffixes)

You can create new words by adding small parts to the beginning (prefix) or the end (suffix) of a root word.

### Prefixes (Change the meaning, often to the opposite)
*   **un- / in- / im- / ir- / il- / dis- (Meaning: Not / Opposite):**
    *   fair $\to$ **un**fair (غير عادل)
    *   visible $\to$ **in**visible (غير مرئي)
    *   polite $\to$ **im**polite (غير مهذب - used before p, b, m)
    *   regular $\to$ **ir**regular (غير منتظم - used before r)
    *   legal $\to$ **il**legal (غير قانوني - used before l)
    *   agree $\to$ **dis**agree (لا يوافق)
*   **re- (Meaning: Again):**
    *   write $\to$ **re**write (يعيد كتابة)
*   **mis- (Meaning: Wrongly / Badly):**
    *   understand $\to$ **mis**understand (يسيء الفهم)
*   **over- (Meaning: Too much):**
    *   work $\to$ **over**work (إرهاق في العمل)
*   **under- (Meaning: Not enough):**
    *   pay $\to$ **under**pay (يدفع أجراً زهيداً)

### Suffixes (Change the word class: noun, verb, adjective)
*   **To form Nouns (الأسماء):**
    *   -tion / -sion: educate $\to$ educa**tion** (تعليم)
    *   -ment: develop $\to$ develop**ment** (تطوير)
    *   -ness: happy $\to$ happi**ness** (سعادة)
    *   -ity: active $\to$ activ**ity** (نشاط)
    *   -er / -or (Person who does the action): teach $\to$ teach**er**, act $\to$ act**or**
*   **To form Adjectives (الصفات):**
    *   -ful (Full of): use $\to$ use**ful** (مفيد)
    *   -less (Without): use $\to$ use**less** (عديم الفائدة)
    *   -ous: danger $\to$ danger**ous** (خطير)
    *   -al: nature $\to$ natur**al** (طبيعي)
    *   -able / -ible (Can be done): accept $\to$ accept**able** (مقبول)
*   **To form Adverbs (الظروف):**
    *   -ly: slow $\to$ slow**ly** (ببطء)

## 2. Synonyms and Opposites (The Word Pairs)

Knowing synonyms (words with similar meanings) and opposites (antonyms) is crucial for the vocabulary matching exercise.

### Common Synonyms (مرادفات)
*   Important = Crucial = Essential = Vital (مهم / ضروري)
*   Benefit = Advantage = Profit (فائدة / ميزة)
*   Drawback = Disadvantage = Negative aspect (عيب / سلبية)
*   Increase = Rise = Go up = Grow (يزيد / يرتفع)
*   Decrease = Fall = Go down = Drop (ينقص / ينخفض)
*   Solve = Resolve = Fix (يحل مشكلة)
*   Protect = Preserve = Safeguard (يحمي / يحافظ على)
*   Fake = Counterfeit = Forged (مزيف / مقلد)

### Common Opposites (أضداد)
*   Legal $\neq$ Illegal (قانوني / غير قانوني)
*   Honest $\neq$ Dishonest (صادق / غير صادق)
*   Ethical $\neq$ Unethical (أخلاقي / غير أخلاقي)
*   Increase $\neq$ Decrease (زيادة / نقصان)
*   Success $\neq$ Failure (نجاح / فشل)
*   Wealthy (Rich) $\neq$ Poor (غني / فقير)
*   Modern $\neq$ Ancient / Traditional (حديث / قديم)

## 3. Phrasal Verbs (The Tricky Pairs)

A verb + a preposition/adverb that creates a completely new meaning.

*   **Give up:** Stop doing something (يستسلم / يقلع عن). *He gave up smoking.*
*   **Look forward to:** Be excited about the future (يتطلع إلى). *I look forward to seeing you.*
*   **Carry out:** Do or complete a task/experiment (ينفذ / يقوم بـ). *Scientists carried out research.*
*   **Take over:** Take control of something (يتولى السيطرة). *The new manager took over the company.*
*   **Set up:** Start a business or organization (يؤسس / ينشئ). *They set up a new charity.*
*   **Bring about:** Cause something to happen (يحدث / يسبب). *The internet brought about huge changes.*
